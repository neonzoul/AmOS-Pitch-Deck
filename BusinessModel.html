Business Model

Business Model & Unit Economics

How AutomateOS makes money, scales margins, and ties to milestones.

Revenue Streams

Ops (PAYG)

Tiered from $0.010/op → $0.008 at volume. Users pay per workflow operation executed, with discounts at scale.

Samantha (AI add-on)

$20/user/month — recommend → run → explain → monitor workflows. Optional like Notion AI.

Marketplace

One-time purchases; 0% fee early, then 10–20% rev-share later. Creator-first approach.

Margins by Stream

Stream Gross Margin Notes
Ops PAYG ~70% → 75–80% Autoscaling, caching, infra deals
Samantha ~65% → 75–80% Multi-model routing, caching, selective fine-tune
Marketplace ~90% steady Digital goods; minus payment rails

Unit Economics Snapshot

  • Blended ARPU: ~$8/mo (pre-AI wide adoption; rises with AI & templates)
  • CAC (community-led): Target <$30 per paying user
  • Payback: <3 months at $8 ARPU
  • LTV: $192–240 (24–30 mo × $8), higher when AI + Templates attach
  • LTV:CAC ratio: Target >6:1

Sensitivity (Key Levers)

Scenario Impact
LLM cost ↓ 30% +10–15 pts Samantha GM
AI attach +20pp +30–60% ARPU uplift
Template AOV +$20 +5–10% blended revenue/user

ARR Potential

  • SAM (12–24m): ~1.5M target pros × $8/mo ≈ $144M/yr
  • SOM (36m, 10% share): ~150k paying ⇒ $14–18M ARR

Milestones Tie-in

  • M6 (0–6M): MVP parity, iPad Flow Board, One-Click Share; 1k installs, 50 paying
  • M12 (6–12M): Creator Analytics, 10k installs, marketplace alpha; 500–1k paying
  • M18 (12–18M): Samantha v1, 100+ active creators, 50k installs; 2–4k paying
  • M24 (18–24M): Marketplace rev-share live; 6–10k paying; AI attach ≥40%; ARR $5–8M

Revenue model scales in lockstep with these milestones.
